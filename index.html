My journey so far.

My journey into the world of web development has been a thrilling adventure. I've dedicated myself to mastering HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and have become proficient in using tools like Webpack and npm to streamline my development workflow.

As a front-end developer, I get a kick out of creating websites that not only look cool but work smoothly too. I'm also a strong advocate for web accessibility, believing that the internet should be a welcoming place for everyone, regardless of their abilities. That's why I'm dedicated to learning techniques and strategies that make it easier for people with diverse capabilities to enjoy my projects.
